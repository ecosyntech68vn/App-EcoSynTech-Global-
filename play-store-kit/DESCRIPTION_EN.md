## Full description (4000 chars max)

🌱 **EcoSynTech Farm OS — Field companion for modern farmers**

EcoSynTech Farm OS is a mobile app for **farmers working directly in the field**, connecting to the EcoSynTech precision agriculture OS (WebLocal Core + GAS Cloud) deployed at many small-to-mid farms in Vietnam.

**Design philosophy:**
- **Offline-first** — works perfectly when signal drops, auto-sync when back online
- **Frugal** — tiny bundle <15MB, battery-efficient, data-efficient
- **Human-centric** — simple UI, large fonts, one-hand operation
- **Safety → Reliability → Recovery** — uncompromising priority chain

**5 core features V1.0:**

📊 **Sensor Dashboard** — Real-time monitoring of temperature, humidity, pH, EC, water level per zone. Color-coded threshold alerts.

🚨 **Alert Center** — Receive farm alerts, swipe to acknowledge, filter by status. Real-time unread badge.

📝 **Operation Log** — Quickly log irrigation/fertilizer/pest/harvest activities with photo attachment. Offline storage, auto-sync.

✅ **Task List** — Get assigned tasks from manager, grouped by status, one-tap mark complete.

⚙️ **Flexible Settings** — Auto-fallback LAN → Cloud, change server URL, manage sync queue.

**No ads · No trackers · No data selling.**

The app communicates **only with your own server** (WLC running on your farm + your deployed GAS Cloud). EcoSynTech Global does not operate intermediate servers, does not receive your data.

**Requirements:**
- Android 8.0+ (API 26)
- WLC v6.0 or GAS V10.3 deployed on your farm backend
